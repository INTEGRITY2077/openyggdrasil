# Intake Workflow

Use this workflow when a piece of knowledge is worth making durable in the
public vault.

1. Identify the public-safe source reference.
2. Decide whether the material is durable, reusable, and hard to re-derive.
3. Create or update the relevant page under `concepts/`, `entities/`,
   `comparisons/`, or `queries/`.
4. Preserve symbolic source/provenance fields instead of copying raw provider
   sessions.
5. Update `index.md`.
6. Append a concise entry to `log.md`.

Do not use vault intake for temporary operator chatter, provider raw transcript
storage, credential material, or private development history.
